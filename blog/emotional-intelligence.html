Emotional Intelligence

13 September 2021

Emotional intelligence (EQ) is the ability to perceive, manage, and regulate emotions in positive ways.
It is different from IQ in that:
IQ tests measure your ability to solve problems, use logic, and grasp or communicate complex ideas.
While EQ tests measure your ability to recognize emotion in yourself and others, and to use that awareness to guide your decisions.

EQ can be used to defuse conflicts, turn intention into action and make informed decisions that relate to your own values. It's highly valued in employees and just helps you be an all round better human.
